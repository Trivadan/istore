  • About

  • What is Jenga Payment Gateway?

    Jenga PGW allows merchants to accept payments from their customers online. These customers can make their payments using cards (debit and credit) as well as mobile money providers in sub-saharan Africa.

    Merchants can be able to start accepting these payments by using our “checkout UI” or integrate directly using APIs

  • How is Jenga PGW different than other payment gateways?
    1. Read our guides and tutorials on the Developer Hub (developers.jengapgw.io)
    2. When you’re ready to try out on your application using test or Sandbox you tap on ‘Sign up’ and enter a few details about yourself on JengaHQ
    3. Try out our APIs or checkout UI on your application
    4. When you’re ready accept real payments, provide additional information about you and your company on Jenga HQ
    5. We’ll verify your details and you’ll receive a confirmation mail
    6. You can now accept real payments and now use production credentials on your application
    7. View transactions on JengaHQ

  • Registration and activation

  • How can I start using Jenga PGW?

    You can register on our dashboard at dashboard.jengapgw.io , fill the required details and upload the necessary documents. We will verify your activation form and pass on to you regarding additional requirements, if any, over mail. We will then get your form verified from various banks and you can start accepting payments.

  • Can I signup using a company registered as a sole proprietorship?

    You can sign up as an individual or businesses registered as partnerships or non-profits. When you’re ready to accept real payments you’ll need to provide more information about your business.

  • What documents are required for verification process?

    We require you to upload certificate of incorporation and Tax PIN registration document (if your company is registered outside of Kenya you can upload the relevant VAT registration doc) and provide information about you and the business

  • Is a partnership firm acceptable?

    Yes.

  • Can I sign up?

    You can sign up for a test account, but to perform live transactions, you will need to activate your account by providing additional information.

  • Where can I find details about pricing?

    Pricing details are available at: https://jengapgw.io/pricing/

  • I have very large volume of transactions. Can you offer a discount?

    You definitelystart your integration process, and we can slowly shift you to a custom plan if your transaction volume increase.

  • Accepting Payments

  • What all currencies are currently supported?

    Currently, we support multiple (major) currencies which your customers can make their payments with – including KES, RWF, TZS, UGX, GBP, USD and EUR..

  • Are international credit cards supported?

    Yes. Including American Express whch most aggregators in this region can’t process.

  • Where can I try out the checkout process?

    On your application, you can try a test and a live version. The live version will deduct real money, so be careful.

  • Do you have support for mobile devices?

    Yes, but checkout UI is only on Android and web using our libraries and SDK. However, you can use the APIs to integrate to your application directly

  • What browsers are supported?

    Everything newer than Internet Explorer 8

  • When is the money transferred to the merchant's account?

    Money transfer happens immediately for mobile money and up to 2 working days for the rest. Will get confirmation on this though.

  • Support

  • Do you offer live chat support?

    Yes. Open jengapgw.io or Jenga HQ in any modern web browser on a desktop and click the box at bottom right.

  • Where can I call you?

  • Tech

  • Do you offer SLA guarantees?

    Currently, we don't, but we plan to change this in the future.

  • Can I handle multiple payouts to multiple bank accounts as a marketplace?

    Sure, you can use Jenga HQ route for it.

  • Are you PCI compliant?

    Security is paramount and to this end we are certified with a Level 1 global security standard certification.

  • Do you have integrations for ecommerce platforms?

    Yes, we support integrations on different platforms. Get in touch if you have a need any of these or something else, or just use our API.

  • Can I submit credit card information to my own site and create a charge?

    You will need to be PCI compliant in order to do that. Drop us a mail on support@jengahq.io in this regard and we'll get in touch with you.
